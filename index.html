Team Five's Book Explorer


This site is a one-stop ecommerce search engine that searches over 150 million books for sale. Books that are new, used, and possible to borrow from a library near you.

We save you time and money by searching New York Times Bestsellers Lists and letting you know which booksellers are offering the best prices and selection.

We are heavy readers, and buy several dozen books every year using our own search engine. We take pleasure in advocating for a strong, diverse, bookselling industry.

Dive-in and enjoy!
